about me

Hello! My name is Andy Wade and I am curently a full time student enrolled in DevCodeCamp.
I have always been very curious about how things work. When other kids where trying to get to the last level of Mario Bro. I was taking the cartridge apart to see what it looked like on the inside. One day I decided to take my fathers BB gun apart to see how it worked and I couldn't get it back together, so I put the pieces in a paper bag to try again later. A few days later my father asked where it was, and I handed it to him in the paper bag. I was in BIG trouble! Dear Ole Dad gave me 15 minutes to put it back into working order or else. But that's not what got to me. As I was walking away mother ask him "Don't you think you where a little hard on him?" to which he replied, "Yeah, your right, I don't think he's smart enough to do it... I don't think I'm smart enough to do it either!" CHALLENGE ACCEPTED! I put that thing together faster than a marine fresh out of bootcamp. Ever since that day I've felt there was no problem I could not solve. The challenging ones I did first, the imposible ones are taking a little bit longer.
