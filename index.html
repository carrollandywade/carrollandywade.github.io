Why do we need developers?
History gives many examples of how technology changed civilization throughout time. Words like: the bronze-age, the iron-age, and the space-age get knocked around all the time. Some people believe natural resources are what make a nation like ours prosperous. They will talk about the gold rush, the oil rush as if those where events that launced us into superpower status, and of course there is the industrial revolution. However, everyone seems to overlook the catilyst that began all of those events. In fact I dare say that everytime there is a major advacement in technology or any advancement in society it could never have happened had it not been for one key element that everyone seems to take for granted.

Do you know when the first steam engine was invented? If you said "Umm, around 1712ish..." you were wrong. Did you know it was invented about 2000 years ago! Of course, they didn't have steam locomotives back in ancient Greese so what happened? They had sailboats, why not slap a steam engine on a sailboat and BOOM tell Christopher Colombus to take the day off! Did you know that Nicoli Tesla probably invented voice-radio while Marconi was still working with the Morse code? These guys had technology ahead of their time and yet they did nothing with it, why?

What would you say if I told you that history has it all wrong, that tecnology and resourses are not what define an erra but rather communication! Why did we not have trans-continental railroads in the dark-ages, because the library of Alexandria burned down before someone with an imagination could read about it. Why did Tesla not tell Marconi to take a hike, because it was just the side-effect of one of his experiments and he didn't think it was important. The truth is major inovation always seems to come behind a major breakthrough in communication. For the Ancient world it was paper and sailing vessels that could carry copper and tin. After that it was the Roman road system. Then it was the printing press that allowed early scientist to share notes quicker than ever. The steam engine allowed people for the first time in history to travel long distances safely, before that it took months to cross the attlantic, suddenly they could do it in 2 weeks! The telegraph, radio, telephone, TV, transatlantic flight. All of these things allowed us to share ideas faster than ever before.

Now here we are! Communication his faster and more accessable than ever before. What's that you invented a steam engine, send me a picture. Just discovered oil and don't know what to do with it, click here! The library in Alexandria just burned down, don't worry we backed it up on the server you'll be fine. Honestly, I'm excited, who know's what the next technological age will bring, all because of a dveloper.
